Sort GPS Button

The intention of this button is to provide a sorted list of parks based on proximity to the user in ascending order. It should be used either in or very near Sarasota County Florida. It provides a rough approximation of distance from the user's current location to park location without needing cell service or wifi. This button uses GPS coordinates of your phone to calculate a straight line distance from the user's location to the park. Thus, you may notice differences between driving distance and the calculated straight line distance. For example, if you are at Bayfront Park on Longboat Key, the straight line distance to North Water Tower Park is about 5.7 miles while the driving distance is about 12 miles. This button also renders an arrow to the right of distance. The arrow shows in which direction the park is from the user's current location relative to north (top of the screen).

Offline Use

You can download this webpage and use offline. On Android, using Chrome: 1) tap the three vertical dots at the top of the page; 2) at the top of the modal, tap the downward arrow with the line under it; 3) follow prompts, open it, go back to the three vertical dots, and tap on "add to home screen". The toilet icon should automatically show up as the app icon. It's a work in progress.
